Sourcing Sustainably: Behind Our Supply Chain

Every ingredient we use has a story. We think it’s important to know where things come from and what impact they have before they reach your bathroom shelf.

Palm Oil: The Hard Conversation

Palm oil is common in soap making because it creates a hard bar with stable lather. But conventional palm farming is one of the leading causes of deforestation. We source only RSPO-certified sustainable palm and are actively working to phase it out entirely.

Our Scottish Suppliers

Wherever possible, we source locally. Our lavender comes from a small farm in Perthshire. Our oats are milled in Fife. Keeping our supply chain short means fewer food miles and fresher botanicals.
