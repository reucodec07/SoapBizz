At SoapBizzz, we believe that what you put on your body is just as important as what you put in it. That's why we're committed to the traditional art of cold-process soap making.

## The Science of Suds

Unlike commercial "beauty bars," which are often just solid detergents, cold-pressed soap is a result of a natural chemical reaction called saponification. By mixing high-quality plant oils with an alkali, we create real soap that is naturally rich in glycerin.

> Glycerin is a humectant that draws moisture to your skin. Many commercial brands remove it to sell separately.

## Why Temperature Matters

By keeping our process "cold" (room temperature), we preserve the delicate vitamins and nutrients found in our essential oils and botanical extracts. This results in a bar that doesn't just clean — it actively nourishes your skin.

Our soaps are then "cured" for 4 to 6 weeks. This waiting period allows the bars to become harder, longer-lasting, and creates the signature creamy lather our customers love.
