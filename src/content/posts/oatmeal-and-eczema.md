Colloidal oatmeal has been used for centuries to soothe irritated skin, and modern science backs it up. We include it in several of our bars for exactly that reason.

## What is Colloidal Oatmeal?

Colloidal oatmeal is finely ground oats processed to mix easily into products. When applied to skin, it forms a protective barrier that helps retain moisture and reduce inflammation.

> The FDA recognises colloidal oatmeal as a safe and effective skin protectant.

## Who Benefits Most?

People with eczema, psoriasis, or generally dry skin tend to see the most benefit. Our Oatmeal Honey bar is our gentlest formula — ideal for sensitive skin.
